Tips for Tourists

  • 🎟️ Many ramen shops use a ticket vending machine at the entrance — buy your ticket before sitting down.
  • 💴 Some smaller restaurants are cash only. Keep ¥1,000–¥2,000 notes handy.
  • 🚫 It is completely normal to slurp your noodles loudly — it shows you enjoy the food!
  • 🧴 Toppings like extra chashu (pork), soft-boiled egg (ajitsuke tamago), or extra noodles (kaedama) are usually cheap add-ons.
  • ⏰ Popular shops often have queues at lunch. Arrive early or go at an off-peak time.
